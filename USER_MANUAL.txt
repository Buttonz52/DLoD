Controls:

ESC: close program
P: Pause background audio
ENTER: choose menu option / exit program from win/lose screen
SPACE: play sfx/ lay trap
UP: accelerate
DOWN: reverse
LEFT: Turn left
RIGHT: turn right
B (in menu): Go back to title screen

Notes:

In this milestone we have 1 AI driver implemented who will continuously 
drive towards the player. Traps will spawn above the car and fall down,
on collision they will damage the player.
